NFT Minting

How To Mint a GHE To mint a GooseHonk Exclusive visit our dapp at: https://mint.goosehonk.org/ Minting costs 0.1 BNB Press the connect button to connect your metamask wallet Choose the number of GHE NFT’s and click buy (20 max limit) Your wallet will popup for you to confirm the transaction Once the transaction is confirmed your GHE NFT will be sent to your wallet

How to add/view your NFT in metamask wallet After the nft is minted, you need to find the collectible id in BSCscan by putting in your wallet address and looking for the transaction Copy your wallet address, then go to bscscan.com, paste it into the search, look at list of transactions, find the one that says “mint”.

It may take ten minutes (or more) for BSCscan to index the transaction, so if you do not see it right away just wait a few minutes and refresh. Click on the transaction, then look for “TokenId

[___] ” the number in that part is the collectible id In MetaMask, click on NFTs, paste the contract address, then add that collectible ID, and the NFT will appear Contract address: 0x1b8fad50717ce15fa511967faf443d277221d927
